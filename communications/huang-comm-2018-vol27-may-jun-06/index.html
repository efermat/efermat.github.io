Comparison of the average global exposure of a population induced by a macro 3G network in urban, suburban and rural areas

Authors: Y. Huang, A. Krayni, A. Hadjem, J. Wiart, C. Person, N. Varsier

Source: FERMAT, Volume 27, Communication 6, May.-Jun., 2018

Abstract: User profiles depend on the considered geographical area, small differences exist among them  We evaluated the average exposure of a population to EMF induced by a 3G macro network in three different geographical areas (dense urban, suburban and rural), with respect to different user profiles  EI calculated for the population in urban area is the highest  Uplink exposure has important weight to the global exposure for a 3G network  Downlink exposure in urban area is important because of the high base stations density

Index Terms: Exposure Index (EI), Average global exposure of a population, Network traffic planning tools
