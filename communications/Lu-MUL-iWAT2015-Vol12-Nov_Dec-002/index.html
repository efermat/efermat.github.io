A Polarization-Reconfigurable Rectenna for Microwave Power transmission

Authors: Ping Lu,Xuesong Yang,Jia-Lin Li and Bingzhong Wang

Source: FERMAT,Volume 12,Communication,Nov-Dec,2015

Abstract: Apolarization reconfigureable retenna which consists of a polarization reconfigurable planar monopole antenna and a planar rectifying circuit, is proposed for microwave power transmission (MPT). The planar monopole is designed with one linear polarization (LP) and two circular polarizations (CPs). The rectifying circuit is connected to antenna with a 50 ohm microstrip line.The rectenna can harvest the RF power from 5.07 GHz to 5.86GHz and convert it to DC power efficiently from 40.2% to 57.4% with input power of 16.5 dBm. Simulated results are confirmed through the experiment.
