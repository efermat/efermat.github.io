Authors: George Shaker, Gholamreza Rafi, Nagula Sangary, and Safieddin Safavi-Naeini

Source: FERMAT, Volume 8, Article 1, Jan-Feb., 2015

This design methodology is particularly useful when the available real estate is very limited, and modifying the ground plane is not a viable practical option- a situation that is typically encountered when designing smartphones. In addition, the design guidelines are applicable for multi-element arrays, resulting in the possible realization of large compact arrays, which is desirable for saving chipset cost when targeting mm-wave applications.
